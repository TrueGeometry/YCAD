# Pipe Flange Design Script
/view top

# 1. Main Body
/parametric cylinder 50 50 10
/tag_last FlangeBody

# 2. Center Bore
/parametric cylinder 15 15 20
/tag_last CenterBore
/subtract @FlangeBody @CenterBore
/tag_last FlangeStep1

# 3. Bolt Hole Pattern
/parametric cylinder 5 5 20
/move 30 0 0
/tag_last HoleCutter
/pattern circ 4 360 y

# 4. Subtract Holes
/subtract @FlangeStep1 @HoleCutter @HoleCutter_c1 @HoleCutter_c2 @HoleCutter_c3
/tag_last FinalFlange

# 5. Finish
/setprop @FinalFlange material steel
/view iso
/view fit
